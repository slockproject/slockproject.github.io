Slock

Slock is a browser extension to effortlessly provide E2E encryption on Facebook Messenger.

Truly Secure Communication


Slock uses the same world-class PGP encryption trusted by security conscious users and orgnanizations for securing their communications for years.

Stupidly Simple Setup


Slock is designed to just work. Install our Chrome extension and follow the simple setup. You'll be securely communicating in minutes.

Always Open and Free


Digital privacy is a right, and Slock is here to help you maintain it. Slock is open source, which means it's supported and improved by a vibrant community of developers passionate about helping everyone communicate safely and securely.
